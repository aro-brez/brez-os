# APPENDIX A: KEY PRINCIPLES

## The Credibility Playbook (From Nick)

1. **Underpromise, Overdeliver**
   - Say 6 months, deliver in 5
   - Never promise what you can't guarantee

2. **First to Speak Loses**
   - Send the email
   - Wait 48 hours
   - Let them respond first

3. **Pattern Beats Promise**
   - One commitment kept means little
   - Consistent delivery creates trust
   - Trust creates leverage

4. **Execute Regardless**
   - Send the note
   - If no response, proceed with default
   - Don't let their inaction stop your action

5. **New Money Rules**
   - Never hamstring future fundraising
   - Keep obligations general, not conditional
   - Preserve optionality
