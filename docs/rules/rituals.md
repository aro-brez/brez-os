## Team Onboarding Questions (One Per Department)

To avoid noise, ask **one sharp question** to each department:

| Department | Question |
|------------|----------|
| **Growth** | "What single lever most improves contribution margin in your work?" |
| **Marketing/Creative** | "What causes the most rework or unclear direction?" |
| **Retail/Sales** | "What blocks velocity fastest at the shelf?" |
| **Ops/Finance** | "Where does complexity hurt cash the most?" |
| **Customer Success** | "What makes customers not come back?" |
| **Product** | "Which SKU creates the most pain per dollar?" |

### Synthesis Rule
AI synthesizes themes from answers, does NOT quote verbatim. Look for:
- Patterns across departments
- Contradictions that reveal gaps
- Quick wins vs structural issues

## The Boring Checklist

Before any meeting or report, verify:

- [ ] Data is current (no stale numbers)
- [ ] KPIs match source of truth
- [ ] Decisions have clear owners
- [ ] Deadlines are specific dates
- [ ] Success metrics are defined
- [ ] NO-gate has been applied to recommendations

If any checkbox fails â†’ fix before proceeding.

---

## Anti-Patterns to Kill

**In Meetings**:
- Discussing topics not on agenda
- Leaving without clear next actions
- Relitigating past decisions without new data
- "Status update" without decisions needed

**In Reports**:
- Vanity metrics (followers, impressions)
- Data without context or comparison
- Recommendations without CM impact
- Dashboards nobody looks at

**In Decisions**:
- Deciding without deadline
- Deciding without success metric
- Reverting without documenting why
- Paralysis by analysis
