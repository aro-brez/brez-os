# BREZ Brand Guidelines — Feel-Good Tonics

## Brand Essence

**Tagline**: Feel-good Tonics™

**Subhead**: Mushroom & cannabis-powered drinks crafted for how you want to feel

**Core Promise**: Drink better, feel better

**Brand Philosophy**: BREZ is not the cure. You are. BREZ is a tool that reminds people of their own agency.

## Voice & Tone

### Voice Characteristics
- **Confident but not cocky** — We know our products work, without being preachy
- **Playful but purposeful** — Fun language, serious about wellness
- **Inclusive and inviting** — Everyone is welcome to feel better
- **Transparent and honest** — No hidden ingredients, no BS marketing
- **Empowering** — You're in control of how you feel

### Tone Guidelines
```
DO:
├── Speak like a friend who discovered something amazing
├── Use conversational, accessible language
├── Focus on feelings and experiences, not just features
├── Be optimistic and possibility-focused
├── Celebrate choice and agency
└── Make wellness feel approachable, not clinical

DON'T:
├── Sound preachy or lecture people about health
├── Use fear-based messaging
├── Over-promise or make medical claims
├── Sound corporate or sterile
├── Shame other beverages (wine, beer, etc.)
└── Be exclusionary or judgmental
```

## Visual Identity

**Headlines**: Syne Extra Bold
- Use for hero statements, product names, section headers
- Strong, modern, distinctive
- All caps for impact moments

**Body & UI**: PP Fragment Sans Regular
- Clean, readable, contemporary
- Use for descriptions, buttons, navigation
- Good at all sizes

DEEP PURPLE

### UI Principles
1. **Dark mode primary** — Matches dreamy aesthetic
2. **Gradient accents** — Use for cards, headers, emphasis
3. **Lime for actions** — All CTAs use the signature lime green
4. **Subtle glow effects** — Reinforce ethereal brand feel
5. **Smooth animations** — Everything should feel fluid

### Component Styling
```
Buttons:
- Primary: Lime green background, dark text
- Secondary: Transparent with lime border
- Rounded corners (8-12px)
- Subtle hover glow

Cards:
- Dark purple-navy background
- Subtle gradient overlay
- Soft shadow/glow
- Rounded corners (12-16px)

Icons:
- Line style preferred
- Can use gradient fills for emphasis
- Match accent colors contextually

Progress/Charts:
- Use gradient fills (purple → pink → lime)
- Glow effects on data points
- Animated when possible
```

## Writing Guidelines

### Headlines
- Keep it short (3-7 words)
- Lead with feeling or outcome
- Can break grammar rules for impact
- Use Syne Extra Bold

### Body Copy
- Conversational, not corporate
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Active voice always
- Benefits over features

### CTAs
- Action-oriented verbs
- Personal ("Get your..." vs "Get the...")
- Create curiosity when appropriate
- Examples: "Find your flow", "Start feeling better", "Discover your state"

### Product Descriptions
- Lead with the feeling
- Then the occasion/use case
- Then the functional ingredients
- Keep it scannable

## Social Media Voice

### Platform Nuances

### Response Guidelines
- Always respond with warmth
- Match the energy of the message
- Turn complaints into conversations
- Celebrate customer wins publicly
- Never be defensive

## Brand Don'ts

**Never**:
- Make unsubstantiated health claims
- Compare negatively to competitors by name
- Use fear-based messaging
- Sound preachy about lifestyle choices
- Make cannabis/mushroom jokes that trivialize
- Use imagery that could be seen as promoting intoxication
- Promise specific medical outcomes

**Always**:
- Lead with how the product makes you FEEL
- Respect customer intelligence
- Maintain warmth and inclusivity
- Stay compliant with regulations
- Represent diverse experiences authentically
